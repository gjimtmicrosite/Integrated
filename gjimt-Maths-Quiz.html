BCA Maths Readiness Quiz

Screening quiz for students interested in BCA, especially those who may not have studied Mathematics in Class 12th. The quiz uses Vedic Maths-oriented questions on rapid multiplication, squaring, fast addition/subtraction, division tricks, and number patterns.

Student Details

How this quiz works

  • 10 questions are picked randomly from a pool of 30 questions.
  • Difficulty split: 3 Easy, 4 Medium, 3 Hard.
  • Each question has a 60-second timer.
  • If time runs out, the quiz auto-moves to the next question.
  • Final recommendation is based on your percentage score.
Tip: This test is designed to check maths readiness for BCA and identify whether a student needs extra coaching support.

Topics Covered

This quiz is inspired by the SkillX Vedic Maths workshop offering and checks practical mental-maths readiness for students entering professional programs.
