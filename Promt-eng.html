Participant & Workshop Details

How this quiz works

  • 10 questions are picked randomly from a pool of 50 Prompt Engineering questions.
  • Mix of basic and medium-difficulty questions on writing effective prompts and guiding AI tools.
  • Each question has a 60-second timer; if time runs out, it auto-moves ahead.
  • Scores of 50% and above receive a downloadable Certificate of Completion for this 5-day workshop.
This is not a rejection test. It helps the SkillX team understand how confidently you can use AI tools for learning and where guided support is needed.

What this quiz checks

The quiz is aligned with the SkillX Prompt Engineering module and tests your ability to create clear, structured prompts, control AI outputs, and use AI tools effectively and responsibly.

Prompt Writing Basics Structured Prompt Design Role-Based Prompting Instruction Clarity Output Optimization Prompt Refinement Techniques Ethical Use of AI Prompts Real-World Prompt Applications

Outcome Bands & Next Step

  • 9–10 correct: Excellent – Advanced Prompt Engineer. Able to design clear, structured, and highly effective prompts to generate accurate and optimized AI outputs.
  • 7–8 correct: Good – Strong Prompt Understanding. Can create useful prompts and guide AI effectively with minor improvements needed.
  • 5–6 correct: Fair – Needs Practice. Recommended: more hands-on practice in writing structured prompts and refining AI outputs.
  • 0–4 correct: Support Recommended. Repeat Prompt Engineering module and focus on basics like clarity, instructions, and simple prompt design.
