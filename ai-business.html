SkillX AI for Business Decision-Making Quiz

End-of-workshop quiz to check how confidently you can use AI tools for data-driven decision-making, business analysis, customer insights, and strategic problem-solving.
Your score and feedback will guide your next SkillX business and AI pathway.

Participant & Workshop Details

How this quiz works

  • 10 questions are picked randomly from a pool of 50 AI for Business Decision-Making questions.
  • Mix of basic and medium-difficulty questions about using AI tools ethically and effectively.
  • Each question has a 60-second timer; if time runs out, it auto-moves ahead.
  • Scores of 50% and above receive a downloadable Certificate of Completion for this 5-day workshop.
This is not a rejection test. It helps the SkillX team understand how confidently you can use AI tools for learning and where guided support is needed.

What this quiz checks

The quiz is aligned with the SkillX AI for Business Decision-Making module and checks how responsibly and effectively you use AI tools in your Business.

Data Analysis using AI Customer Insights Sales & Demand Forecasting Pricing Strategy Risk Analysis Decision-Making with AI Ethical Use of AI in Business Automation in Business Tasks

Outcome Bands & Next Step

  • 9–10 correct: Excellent – Data-Driven Decision Maker. Able to use AI insights effectively for business strategy, analysis, and smart decision-making.
  • 7–8 correct: Good – Strong Business AI Understanding. Can apply AI in decision-making with minor guidance and improvement.
  • 5–6 correct: Fair – Needs Practical Application. Recommended: more practice in using AI for real business scenarios and data interpretation.
  • 0–4 correct: Support Recommended. Repeat AI for Business Decision-Making module and focus on basics of data-driven thinking and AI usage.
