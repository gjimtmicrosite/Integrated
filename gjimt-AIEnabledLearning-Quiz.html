SkillX AI-Enabled Learning Quiz

End-of-workshop quiz to check how confidently you can use AI tools for learning, note-making, summarisation and project support. Your score and feedback will guide your next SkillX pathway.

Participant & Workshop Details

How this quiz works

  • 10 questions are picked randomly from a pool of 50 AI-Enabled Learning questions.
  • Mix of basic and medium-difficulty questions about using AI tools ethically and effectively.
  • Each question has a 60-second timer; if time runs out, it auto-moves ahead.
  • Scores of 50% and above receive a downloadable Certificate of Completion for this 5-day workshop.
This is not a rejection test. It helps the SkillX team understand how confidently you can use AI tools for learning and where guided support is needed.

What this quiz checks

The quiz is aligned with the SkillX AI-Enabled Learning module and checks how responsibly and effectively you use AI tools in your studies.

Prompt Basics Summarisation & Notes Idea Generation Ethical Use of AI Avoiding Plagiarism Checking AI Output Subject-Specific Help Time Management with AI

Outcome Bands & Next Step

  • 9–10 correct: Excellent – AI-Enabled, Safe & Smart. Ready for deeper AI + domain modules.
  • 7–8 correct: Good – Strong AI Learning Habits. Ready to combine AI with communication and presentations.
  • 5–6 correct: Fair – Needs Guided Practice. Recommended: AI practice/revision and Digital Power Skills basics.
  • 0–4 correct: Support Recommended. Repeat AI-Enabled Learning / Digital Power Skills foundation batch.
